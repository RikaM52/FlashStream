Privacy Policy

FlashStream is designed differently. Your personal preferences stay on your device. We only see anonymous patterns that help us make the site better.

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Our approach to privacy

  • Your watchlist, ratings, and personal preferences never leave your device
  • We do not send promotional emails or newsletters
  • We do not use pop-up ads or interrupt your experience
  • We do not ask for your name, email address, or payment information
  • We do not track you across other websites

Anonymous usage patterns that help improve FlashStream

To make FlashStream faster, more reliable, and more useful, we look at anonymous patterns of how people use the site. This information is grouped together so we only see patterns across many users, not individual activity. This is similar to how a store might notice that many customers browse the science fiction section — they learn what people want without knowing who those people are.

How this helps you

  • When searches return no results, we know what content to add
  • When certain features are slow, we know what to optimize
  • When recommendations miss the mark, we improve them
  • When something breaks, we see it and fix it quickly

What we observe (always anonymous)

  • Search and filter patterns — which combinations of filters people use, and when searches return no results
  • Content engagement — which titles people hover over or click on (never your specific watchlist)
  • Trailer interaction — whether trailers are played, how long they are watched
  • Outbound clicks — which streaming platforms people click through to (Netflix, Viki, iQIYI, and others)
  • General location — country level only, so we can show relevant streaming options
  • Device type — whether you use mobile, tablet, or desktop, so we know what to optimize
  • Episode patterns — which episodes people watch or stop watching, which helps storytellers understand pacing
  • Feature usage — which parts of the site are most used, so we know where to focus improvements
  • Watchlist patterns — which titles are saved most often (aggregated counts, never individual lists)
  • Returning usage — a hashed, non-reversible identifier helps us understand how often people come back to FlashStream

What we do not observe

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your full IP address (it is shortened before we see it)
  • Your precise location (only country level)
  • Your private watchlist contents or personal ratings
  • Your browsing activity on other websites

How we protect anonymous information

Before any usage information is stored, we apply several protections to ensure it cannot be linked back to you:

  • IP addresses are shortened — for example, 192.168.1.45 becomes 192.168.1.x
  • Detailed browser version information is removed
  • Timestamps are rounded to the nearest hour
  • Session identifiers are changed daily
  • We never store email addresses, names, or contact information

Your choices

You are always in control of your information.

  • View your data — Open your browser's developer tools, go to Application, then Local Storage. All your data is there in plain text.
  • Delete your data — Go to Settings and select "Reset all preferences", or clear your browser storage.
  • Export your data — Download your watchlist as CSV or JSON from Settings or the Watchlist page.
  • Export your data (enhanced) — Download your watchlist and preferences as JSON/CSV from Settings → Export.
  • Manage analytics consent — Use the consent banner at the bottom of this page or go to Settings → Privacy.

Security measures

  • All data transmission uses TLS encryption (the same standard used by banks)
  • IP addresses are shortened before any storage
  • Browser details are stripped of version information
  • Timestamps are rounded to the nearest hour
  • Cloudflare provides DDoS protection and edge security

Children's privacy

FlashStream is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child has used FlashStream, please contact us at privacy@flashstream.app and we will help remove any related information.

What FlashStream does not do

  • FlashStream does not host, stream, or download any video content
  • FlashStream does not provide playback controls, download buttons, or subtitle customization
  • All "Watch Now" buttons link to third-party streaming platforms such as Netflix, Viki, and iQIYI
  • You must have your own active subscriptions with those services
