About FlashStream

A free, privacy‑first platform for discovering what to watch worldwide — no account required.

Our Mission

FlashStream exists to make great film and television discoverable by everyone — regardless of language, country, or streaming budget. The world produces extraordinary stories from every corner of the globe, and too many of them go unseen simply because discovery tools are designed for a Western, English‑speaking audience.

We believe in the power of cinema to build empathy, spark curiosity, and bring joy. FlashStream is our contribution to making that easier.

What Makes FlashStream Different

🌐

Truly Global

Korean drama, Japanese anime, French cinema, Indian Bollywood, Thai horror — we celebrate film from everywhere. Filter by 20+ original languages and 30+ countries.

🔒

Privacy First

No account required. Your watchlist, ratings, and preferences are stored only on your device. We never sell data. Analytics are opt‑in only.

🎚️

60+ Filter Dimensions

Filter by mood, thematic keywords, narrative structure, pacing, language, runtime, certification, platform, and more. Find exactly what you're in the mood for.

🧘

Wellbeing Tools

Session timer, break reminders, content trigger warnings, and emotional safety modes. Watch mindfully.

📴

Works Offline

Installable as a PWA. Your watchlist is always available, even without internet.

💸

Always Free

No paywalls, no premium tiers for core features. Sustained by optional, consent‑based advertising.
