Frequently asked questions

Is FlashStream really free?

Yes, completely free. No paywalls, no premium tiers. Every feature is available to everyone, forever. We are sustained by optional, consent-based advertising.

Do you host videos or stream content?

No. FlashStream is a discovery platform only. We help you find where to watch, but all "Watch Now" buttons take you to official streaming platforms like Netflix, Viki, or iQIYI. You need your own subscriptions with those services.

Why should I add tags to movies and dramas?

Tags help other viewers find exactly what they're looking for. When you add a tag like "Slow Burn" or "Satisfying Ending" to a drama, you help thousands of people discover content that matches their mood. It's a small effort that makes a big difference for the community.

Do I need an account?

No. FlashStream works without an account. Your watchlist and ratings are saved in your browser. If you clear your browser data, your watchlist will be cleared — so you may want to export it from Settings.

How do you protect my privacy?

Your watchlist, ratings, and personal preferences never leave your device. We never ask for your name or email. Analytics are opt-in only. You can read our full Privacy Policy for details.

Why are some dramas missing?

We continuously add new content. If something is missing, go to the Community page and suggest it. We prioritize titles that multiple users request.
